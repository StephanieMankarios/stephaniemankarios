You Have 36 525 Days To Live

In my early twenties, I attended Toast Masters (an international organization aimed at helping people develop their communication and leadership skills), where I met a great friend.

This friend always had a lesson for me: LIVE LIFE TO THE FULLEST.

He used to tell me "If you live to be 100 years old, that means that you will have 36 525 days to live on this earth."

That doesn't seem like much right?

According to statista, the average life expectancy in North America is 77 years for males, and 81 years for females.

That's only 28 124 days for men, and 29 585 days for women.

What is the point of knowing this? Well friend, I want to enthusiastically encourage you to live your life with MEANING.

Life is too short to be doing anything other than what makes your heart feel whole.

Is it really worth it to be living a half-lived-life?

Everyone's definition of living to the fullest will look different.

For some, it might mean to live life in an adventurous way, travelling, exploring, taking risks, having fun.

For others it might mean to have 3 kids and be dedicated to giving them the best childhood possible.

For others it might mean to build schools and hospitals and spread awareness about a cause they feel strongly about.

And for another, it might mean to become an excellent chef and give people a gustatory experience of a lifetime.

What have you ALWAYS wanted to do, but have pushed aside because : it's too scary, too expensive, you're too busy, there are too many responsibilities, etc?

NOW is the time. Yes, NOW!

If not now, when?

Let's not fall into the trap of " when I finish school, when I have more money, when the kids move out, when I feel better, when I retire, when there's no time."

There will always be a reason not to follow what our heart desires most.

I invite you to write a list, of all the things you would like to be/have/do/experience.

Stick this list in your bedroom or somewhere where you will see it several times a day.

Begin to visualize yourself ALREADY living/doing/having/being those things.

Experience the sensations of what it feels like, what it looks like, what it tastes like.

Begin to live as if you are already there.

VISUALIZATION my friends, is a very powerful tool.

Do this for you.

You deserve to live your life to the FULLEST.
