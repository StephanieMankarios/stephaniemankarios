Those limiting beliefs are thoughts, and thoughts can be changed.

Have you ever asked yourself why you believe your own thoughts?

No, but really, what makes them true? The fact that you thought them? The fact that they exist?

The amazing thing is that our thoughts create our feelings, and our feelings feed into our thoughts. It's a cycle. So, the only way to interrupt that cycle, is to consciously change our thoughts.

What would happen if you allowed yourself to believe that everything is possible? Even if the world around you doesn't agree, or holds you back, or doesn't have the same drive and desire as you. All that matters is that YOU do.
